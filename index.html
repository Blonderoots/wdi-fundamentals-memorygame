Instructions

There are no losers in this game! This is to test your concentration so let loose and have fun.

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory Pelmanism, Shinkeisuijaku, Pexso, or Paris, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
